About Me

My Introduction

As an Embedded Engineer, I build end-to-end embedded systems—from schematic and PCB design to firmware programming, sensor integration, and IoT connectivity—across Arduino, ESP8266/ESP32, STM32, and ARM platforms. I work in Embedded C/C++ with real-time protocols (MQTT, HTTP, Modbus) and design custom PCBs in EasyEDA and Altium, optimizing for low power and performance under RTOS with robust device drivers. My experience spans real-time data acquisition and wireless modules (Wi‑Fi, Bluetooth, LoRa), and I prototype and validate quickly using Tinkercad and Proteus. I’ve delivered IoT automation, robotics, sensor-based monitoring, and industrial control solutions. Driven by curiosity and problem-solving, I aim to bridge electronics and intelligent software to create reliable, scalable, and user-friendly products.
